Data accessed: 1/17/2024
Data was downloaded here: https://sage.nelson.wisc.edu/data-and-models/datasets/crop-calendar-dataset/

Visualization of the data can be accessed here: https://asheshwor.shinyapps.io/cropcal/

Associated publication: Sacks, W.J., D. Deryng, J.A. Foley, and N. Ramankutty (2010). Crop planting dates: an analysis of global patterns. Global Ecology and Biogeography 19, 607-620. DOI: 10.1111/j.1466-8238.2010.00551.x.

Winter wheat data for US states were extracted by: 
1) Nation code: 5
2) Crop.name.in.orignial.data: Wheat - winter
3) The month of Plant.median and Harvest.median was used to define the growing season for winter wheat in each state.
